About Ghana

Ghana, known as the "Gateway to Africa," was the first African nation to gain independence from colonial rule. It is celebrated for its stable democracy, rich cultural heritage, and warm, welcoming people.

From the historic Cape Coast Castle to the bustling markets of Accra and the wildlife of Mole National Park, Ghana offers a diverse and unforgettable experience.

Capital: Accra

Official Language: English

Population: Approximately 34 million

Currency: Ghana Cedi (GHS)
